# friends — ad-hoc personal contacts

The cohort folder for testers the founder personally invited
outside the formal alpha-001/alpha-002 cycles. Same schema as
[`alpha-001/TEMPLATE.md`](../alpha-001/TEMPLATE.md); the only
real difference is that *friends* signal **less** in
[`alpha_report.md`](../../alpha_report.md) — they share a context
(they know the founder, they want the project to succeed) that
strangers do not, so their reports tilt charitable.

The founder persona from
[`ALPHA_001_RUNBOOK.md`](../../ALPHA_001_RUNBOOK.md) lives here
unless a maintainer joins (in which case they move to their own
cohort, not friends).

The Phase-5K rule for friends-cohort entries:

- still **founder-assigned handles** (never first names, never
  PII);
- still **metadata only** in the folder;
- if a friend reports something a stranger would not say — *"I
  forced myself to use it because you asked"* — that's the row
  that lives here, not in alpha-001. The whole point of a
  separate cohort is signal-class separation.
