## What to attach with this file

1. **`stats.json`** from `recall stats --export`. See
   [`TRUST.md`](TRUST.md) for what is in it (counts only).
2. **A screenshot of `recall doctor`.** Open Command Prompt, run
   `%LOCALAPPDATA%\Programs\Recall\Recall.exe doctor`, screenshot
   the output.
3. **The extension popup state.** A screenshot of the popup as it
   looked at the end of the week (Connected / Disconnected /
   Missing / Offline / …).

Three small files. That is the entire ask.

## What is *not* attached

The founder explicitly never wants:

- Your event log (`.recall\events\*.jsonl`).
- Your indexed folder list.
- Screenshots that include filenames, URLs, queries.

If you accidentally screenshot one of those, redact before sending.
The founder will refuse to look at non-export content — the
boundary is part of the trust ledger.

## Where to send

is no Recall account, no upload form, no "submit feedback" button.
A continuity tool with no telemetry needs the loop to be human.
