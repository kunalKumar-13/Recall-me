## What Recall is, in one paragraph

Recall is a **local-first continuity layer** for your own thinking.
It watches files, browser tabs, and searches *on your machine* and
later answers *"what was I in the middle of?"* — by opening the
right files, tabs, and chats again. Nothing leaves your computer.

If that sentence didn't land, [`SAMPLE_WORKFLOW.md`](SAMPLE_WORKFLOW.md)
shows it.

## What this alpha covers

- **Windows 10/11.** Verified install path; the one we ask you to test.
- **macOS.** Not yet — see [`LIMITATIONS.md`](LIMITATIONS.md).
- **Linux.** Source only.

## What we want from you

One honest week of normal use, then `FEEDBACK.md` filled out. That
is the whole ask. You are not testing features; you are answering
*"did this earn a place on my machine?"*

## What we never ask for

- Your files. Your URLs. Your search history. Your chats.
- A login. An email. A device id.
- A reason. *"It felt off"* is a complete piece of feedback.

The contract is [`TRUST.md`](TRUST.md) — read it before granting
folder access on day 1.
